what it does

A friendly box of fretboard tricks

Everything you need to map out a song — without fighting your software.

Place notes by feel

Left-click to drop a dot. Hold a modifier and you get a square, a triangle, or a wee one for passing tones.

♯

Barres that just happen

Put dots on neighbouring strings at the same fret and a barre appears. Split, merge and recolour it however you like.

✎

Labels for humans

Tag any dot with a two-character note — finger numbers, intervals, anything that helps you remember.

X for the muted ones

Right-click an empty spot to mark a string as muted or open. No more guessing which strings stay quiet.

Print-ready PDFs

Export crisp diagrams with Ctrl+P. Hand them to your band, pin them to the fridge, teach a lesson.

Made to fit you

2–12 strings, 1–24 frets, dark or light, ten languages, and every hotkey rebindable. It bends to your habit.

How to play

Left-click Place / remove a dot
Shape buttons Pick circle, triangle, square, or small
Ctrl/Shift+Click Override to square / triangle
Right-click X marker / add a label
Scroll Cycle a dot's colours
Click barre Select it, then ↑/↓ to split
Del / Bksp Remove the hovered dot
Ctrl+Z / Y Undo / redo
