Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

FretTool respects your privacy. The interactive fretboard demo on this website runs entirely in your browser (client-side). No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by FretTool itself when you use the demo.

Data processed by third parties

PayPal

The Support page includes a PayPal donation button. When you make a donation, PayPal (PayPal (Europe) S.à.r.l. & Cie, S.C.A., 22-24 Boulevard Royal, L-2449 Luxembourg) processes your payment on its own infrastructure. PayPal may collect your name, email address, payment details, and IP address in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. FretTool does not receive, store, or process any payment information.

Google Fonts

This site loads web fonts from Google Fonts (Google LLC). When you visit the page, your browser may connect to Google's servers to download the font files. According to Google, no cookies are set by the fonts API. See Google Fonts Privacy FAQ for details.

No cookies

This website does not set any cookies or use any tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts. The demo stores no user data on any server.

No data collection

FretTool does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There is no registration, no login, and no server-side storage of fretboard diagrams.

Your rights

Since no personal data is collected by FretTool, there is nothing to access, rectify, or delete. If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the contact details on the Legal Notice page.

Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
